## Project Overview

This ongoing series explores ceramic forms through digital simulation. Each vessel begins as a mathematical definition — curves, rotations, boolean operations — then passes through a series of physics simulations that mimic the behavior of wet clay on a wheel.

## Process

1. **Parametric Definition:** Base forms created using Geometry Nodes in Blender
2. **Physics Simulation:** Cloth simulation with gravity and collision to simulate throwing
3. **Surface Development:** Procedural glaze materials with layering, drips, and kiln effects
4. **Rendering:** Octane Render with spectral lighting for accurate caustics

## Technical Details

- **Software:** Blender 4.0, Octane Render 2023
- **Polycount:** ~50k–200k per model
- **Texture Resolution:** 4K PBR maps
- **Output:** 5K stills + 4K turntable animations
